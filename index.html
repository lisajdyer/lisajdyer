About Lisa

Emerging from the vibrant music scene of Brighton, UK, I am a singer-songwriter and musician, drawing subtle influences from the indie rock genre and cultivating a distinctive flair for acoustic guitar. My creative process finds inspiration in a myriad of influential artists that range from the ethereal melodies of Lana Del Rey and Birdy, to the sultry rhythms of Vanessa Paradis. My music also resonates with the poignant lyrics and the raw authenticity of Jack Savoretti and Nirvana.

My deep-seated love for music intertwines with an affinity for poetry, shaping a unique soundscape that echoes my soulful tunes. Every chord I strum on my guitar and every lyric I write simply represent my love for music and poetry, and keep me moving forward on my musical journey.
